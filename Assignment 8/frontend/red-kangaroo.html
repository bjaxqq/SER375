Red Kangaroo

Macropus rufus

About

The Red Kangaroo (Macropus rufus) is the largest kangaroo species, native to Australia. Known for its strong hind legs, the red kangaroo can cover large distances by hopping at high speeds.

Red kangaroos are herbivores, primarily grazing on grasses, and are well-adapted to Australia\u2019s dry, arid climate. They can go for long periods without drinking water, extracting moisture from the plants they eat.
