Bunny Harvestman

Metagryne bicolumnata

About

The Bunny Harvestman (Metagryne bicolumnata) is a unique arachnid species found in the Amazon rainforest. Despite its spider-like appearance, it's actually a type of harvestman, distinguished by its unusual body shape that resembles a rabbit's head when viewed from above.

These harvestmen are harmless to humans and play an important role in their ecosystem as both predators and prey. They feed on small insects and decomposing organic matter, while serving as food for larger rainforest creatures.
